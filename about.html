About Grover Furnishers

Crafting comfort and durability in Hari Nagar, New Delhi since 1999.

Est. 1999

A Heritage of Wood & Passion

Grover Furnishers & Decorators was founded on a simple philosophy: furniture should not just occupy space; it should breathe luxury, comfort, and reliability.

For more than two decades, we have remained a trusted name in the Jail Road Furniture Market, Hari Nagar. Our journey began as a humble woodworking workshop catering to custom requests. Today, we manage a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with precision seasoning chambers, wood carving mills, and dust-free polishing booths.

We source premium Indian Teakwood, Rosewood, and high-density timber, ensuring each raw trunk is cured, seasoned, and hand-treated before fabrication. This meticulous process ensures our furniture stays free of warp, decay, and moisture damage for generations.

Why Choose Grover Furnishers?

We do not sell boxed, mass-produced particles. We engineer durable structural works tailored to Indian weather and lifestyles.

Double-Seasoned Timber

We dry our wood to under 12% moisture levels, preventing splits, checks, and twists in changing summer-winter cycles.

Bespoke Room Styling

Every bedroom or office space is unique. We adjust sizes down to the millimeter to maximize your floor layout.

Anti-Termite Treatment

Our raw logs are vacuum pressurized and treated with ecological anti-pest shields, offering 100% protection.

Factory Direct Pricing

By producing everything in-house in our Delhi workshop, we skip broker markups and pass down 25-30% savings to you.

Elite Polishes & Coatings

We use premium PU (Polyurethane) finishes, melamine glosses, and natural oils for water-resistant, shiny surfaces.

Seamless Assembly

We don't leave packages at your gate. Our skilled fitters carry, assemble, and inspect every joint inside your room.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries regarding customization options, order times, delivery areas, and warranties.

Do you customize furniture based on Pinterest or online designs?

Yes, absolutely! We specialize in customized furniture manufacturing. You can bring photos, sketches, or reference links, and our designers will build structural sketches mapping the exact dimensions and materials to manufacture it.

What types of wood do you use for your furniture?

We primarily construct using highly seasoned Indian Teak (Sagwan), Rosewood (Sheesham), and high-grade BWR plywood for internal compartments. All wood goes through drying and termite treatments to ensure long-term stability.

How much time does it take to manufacture a custom bed or sofa set?

Standard manufacturing takes between 10 to 20 days depending on the size and complexity of the polish. Simple beds are prepared within 10 days, while detailed classic carved items or complex modular cabinetry can take up to 3 weeks.

Do you deliver outside Hari Nagar/Jail Road?

Yes, we deliver across the entire Delhi NCR region, including Gurgaon, Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. We use specialized, padded trucks to avoid scratches and dents during transit.

Do your furniture products come with a warranty?

Yes. We offer up to a 5-year warranty on timber integrity, covering problems like warping, wood-boring insects (termites), and joint cracks. Hardware like hinges, hydraulic lifts, and drawer runners are covered under brand manufacturer warranties.

What We Do Best

Grover Furnishers offers a full suite of custom manufacturing and maintenance options to help you shape your living environment.

Restoration & Polishing

Breathe life back into family heirlooms. We offer professional melamine spraying, PU gloss coatings, anti-pest treatment, and joint tightening.

Interior Furnishing Solutions

Safe Delivery & Placement

Custom Furniture Process

Manufacturing custom furniture with us is stress-free. Here is a timeline of how we bring your concepts to reality.

01

1. Consultation

Bring your references or Pinterest pictures to our showroom. We discuss sizes, drawer layouts, and pick out wood types (Teak, Rosewood, Ply).

02

2. Spatial Design

Our interior team creates spatial layouts and selects material finishes (matte veneer, gloss laminate, PU paint, velvet tufting).

03

3. Crafting & Polishing

Our carpenters build the frames, sand the timber, cure the moisture, and coat surfaces in dust-controlled booths.

04

4. Delivery & Assembly

We deliver using padded sheets and set up the cabinets, beds, or wardrobes in your rooms, leveling joints perfectly.
